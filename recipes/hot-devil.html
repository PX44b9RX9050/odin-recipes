Hot devil recipe

Description

The "Hot Devil" is a savory and satisfying comfort food dish that combines seasoned ground beef with creamy mashed potatoes. In this delectable recipe, seasoned ground beef is cooked to perfection with spices, onions, and garlic, creating a flavorful and slightly spicy filling. The mashed potatoes are then layered on top of the beef mixture, creating a comforting and creamy topping. The dish is typically baked until the mashed potatoes turn golden brown and slightly crispy on the edges. The result is a delicious and hearty meal that's sure to please your taste buds with its devilishly good flavors.

Noteworthy, this dish is also called "Hot Lightning"

Ingredients

  • 1 kg floury potatoes
  • 4 apples, f.e. Jonagold
  • 30 gram butter
  • 30 ml cream
  • 50 ml milk
  • small handful of breadcrumbs
  • pepper and salt to taste

Steps

Preparation (15 minutes)

  1. Peel the potatoes and slice them into chunks.
  2. Rinse the potato chunks with cold water.
  3. Add the potato chunks to a medium-sized pan.
  4. Fill the pan with water and add salt.
  5. Bring the pan to a boil.
  6. Once the water is boiling, turn down the heat.
  7. Let the potatoes cook for about 15 minutes.
  8. Depending on the size of the potato chunks, peel 3 apples and cut them into chunks as well.
  9. Add the apple chunks into the pan with the potatoes.
  10. Allow the potatoes and apples to cook together.
  11. Check the potatoes with a fork to see if they are ready.

Recipe (10 minutes)

  1. Drain the potatoes and apples and press them through a fine sieve using the ladle. The easiest way is to get a spoonful of drained potatoes at a time. This way your mashed potatoes will be very smooth without any lumps.
  2. Peel the last apple and cut as fine as you can, stir into the mash, this way the hete bliksem will have a little bite to it. Classic hete bliksem is smooth, but the crunchy part will make a difference, really!
  3. Add butter, cream, and milk to the potato and apple mixture. Add the breadcrumbs and season with pepper and salt.
  4. You can now decide how creamy you like the hete bliksem to be by adding more milk and/or cream. We decided to keep it quite firm.
